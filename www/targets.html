SCST Target Drivers

SCST has target drivers for:

  • Software iSCSI
  • Fibre Channel QLogic 22xx/23xx series
  • Fibre Channel QLogic ISP Chipsets, including 24xx and 25xx
  • Infiniband SCSI RDMA Protocol (SRP)
  • Marvell SAS adapters
  • Emulex lpfc FC/FCoE adapters
  • LSI/MPT adapters (parallel SCSI, including Wide Ultra320, SAS, Fibre Channel)
  • FCoE
  • Local access
  • ...
